PAWSIBILITIES FOR CHANGE

RECOVERY THROUGH RESCUE

OUR MISSION

To support recovery, re-entry and rescue. This is made possible by providing opportunities for training and support; facilitated through pairing shelter dogs with parolees and people in recovery.
