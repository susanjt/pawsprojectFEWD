OUR MISSION

To support recovery, re-entry and rescue. This is made possible by providing opportunities for training and support; facilitated through pairing shelter dogs
with parolees and people in recovery.
This program focuses on life skills and providing work experience
through training and care for shelter and rescue dogs.
